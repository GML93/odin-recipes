Vegan Mexican

Description

Do you hate winter? Do you wish you were under the hot mexican sun?

Do not ask for more, this amazing vegan recipe will bring you directly to Mexico.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Salsa
  • Tortilla chips
  • Onions
  • Peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Avocado
  • Garclic

Steps

  1. Bake the tofu at 350*
  2. Cook the veggies
  3. Make the guacamole
  4. Enjoy!
